Leadership & Career Coach | Transition Coach | Facilitator | Strategic Leader | Engineer | Educator | Extended DISC Practitioner

Philippa Martin

I am a leadership, transition and transformation coach based in Ōtautahi | Christchurch, Aotearoa New Zealand. I support people from around the world to thrive through leadership, life, change and transitions. I support clients to make meaningful changes to realize goals and find balance. My training in Coaching for Transformation, Transition Coaching, Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT), Solution Focused Brief Therapy (SFBT) and Motivational Interviewing allows me to support clients to transform their lives, navigate through transitions and get comfortable with the uncomfortable thoughts and feelings associated with stretch goals, uncomfortable conversations, transitions and transformation. Being an extended DISC practitioner allows me to help clients fast-track self-awareness and understanding of others. I’m also a Mum, Leader, Engineer, Innovator, Educator, and Outdoor Enthusiast. I’m passionate about coaching, strategic leadership, engineering, education, life, nature and more.
